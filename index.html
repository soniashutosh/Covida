Social Distancing

Also known as "Physical Distancing".. means keeping space between yourself and other people outside of your home. To practice social or physical distancing stay at least 6 feet (about 2 arm's length) from other people. Limiting face-to-face contact with others is the best way to reduce the spread of coronavirus disease.
